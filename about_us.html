About us

There is a maternal health crisis worldwide, The truth is that most maternal deaths are actually preventable, as there exists health-care solutions to prevent or manage complications. Team FOETO brings solution to this ever existing problem with the association of expert doctors in ayurveda, gynecologists and social reformers. FOETO has been making sincere efforts to empower humanity for timeless happiness by nurturing the unborn child. We empower hospitals to foster collaborative thinking to further support patient care and drive workplace innovation.
